About Warlord Realm

Warlord Realm is a anarchy survival server. There are no rules, no land claims, no protection systems, and no staff interference in player conflicts. Griefing, raiding, betrayal, alliances, and wars are all part of the experience. The only rule is simple: no hacking or unfair client modifications. Play legit. Originally created for a small group of friends, the server grew naturally as the IP was shared with others. Now it stands as an open world where chaos, strategy, and survival define your story. In Warlord Realm, your power depends on your skill, your alliances, and your ability to survive.

Multiplatform

Supports both Minecraft Java and Bedrock Edition. Play with your friends regardless of the platform they use.

Player-Driven Community

Form alliances, create rivalries, and shape the world together. The story is written by the players.

Joining Process

To join our server, you need to fill out a registration form first. Once the form is approved, the server IP will be sent to you via DM on Discord, WhatsApp, or other social media, and you will be given a special role in the Discord server to access server news and update channels.
